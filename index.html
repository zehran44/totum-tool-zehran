About Totum Tool

Totum Tool is a creative online space where anyone can build digital art — one square at a time. It starts with Pixel Editing, a tool that lets you paint, design, and create shapes using tiny blocks. Made by Zehran, Totum Tool is growing into a full creative platform where you’ll soon find more powerful and fun editing tools for artists and gamers alike.
